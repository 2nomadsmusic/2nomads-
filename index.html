About Us

2 Nomads are DJ/Producers and co-founders of Nomad Camp, Boho Sunday & more brands — they don't book gigs, they sculpt immersive nights. Their nomadic practice is a working argument for music as a universal language: breaking down barriers and building a sense of global unity, one room at a time.

Through their performances and productions they weave a sonic tapestry where traditional melodies dance seamlessly with contemporary beats. Every project they touch is a stage where genre dissolves and diversity becomes the headline.
